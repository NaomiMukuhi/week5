Fun facts about Nairobi

Nairobi is Kenya’s capital city.
In addition to its urban core,
the city has Nairobi National Park,
a large game reserve known for breeding
endangered black rhinos and home to giraffes,
zebras and lions.
Elevation: 1,795 m
Area: 696 km²
Population: 4.397 million (2019)
Area code: 020
